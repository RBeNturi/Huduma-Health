About Huduma Health

Huduma Health is a web application that aims to make healthcare more accessible and affordable for all Kenyans. We provide a platform for patients to register, manage their medical records, and view their bills online. We also provide a platform for doctors to register, manage their patients, and access their medical records.

Features

  • Patient registration and login
  • Doctor registration and login
  • Patient medical record upload
  • Patient bill viewing
  • Doctor patient management
  • Doctor medical record access

Benefits

  • Improved access to healthcare
  • Reduced healthcare costs
  • Improved patient satisfaction
  • Improved doctor efficiency
